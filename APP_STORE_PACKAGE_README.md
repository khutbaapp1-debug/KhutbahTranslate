## 🌍 MARKET-SPECIFIC NOTES

### English Market (USA, UK, Canada, Australia)
- **Target:** English-speaking Muslims, converts, youth
- **Messaging:** "Never miss a word" - accessibility focus
- **Keywords:** Focus on "khutbah translation", "Friday sermon"
- **Competition:** Moderate (general Islamic apps, not translation-specific)

### Hindi/Urdu Market (India, Pakistan, Bangladesh)
- **Target:** South Asian Muslims, bilingual families
- **Messaging:** Educational depth - understanding heritage
- **Keywords:** Mix of Hindi, Urdu, and English terms
- **Competition:** High (many Islamic apps in this region)
- **Cultural note:** Use respectful Arabic transliteration

### French Market (France, Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Senegal)
- **Target:** French-speaking Muslims, North African diaspora
- **Messaging:** Cultural heritage - maintaining connection
- **Keywords:** French Islamic terms + Arabic where appropriate
- **Competition:** Lower (fewer translation-focused apps)
- **Cultural note:** Emphasize community and tradition

## ❓ COMMON QUESTIONS

### Q: Do I need to submit all three markets at once?
**A:** No! Start with English market, get approved, then add localizations later.

### Q: How long until my app is approved?
**A:** iOS: 24-48 hours. Google Play: Few hours to 2 days. First submission may take longer.

### Q: Can I update screenshots after launch?
**A:** Yes! You can update screenshots, descriptions, and keywords anytime without triggering a new review (unless you also upload a new app version).

### Q: What if my app gets rejected?
**A:** Read APP_STORE_SUBMISSION_GUIDE.md "Common Rejection Reasons" section. Fix the issue and resubmit. Most rejections are easily fixable.

### Q: Should I do iOS or Android first?
**A:** Personal preference. iOS has higher-paying users but stricter review. Android has larger user base but more device fragmentation. Consider doing both simultaneously.

### Q: How much will this cost?
**A:**
- Apple Developer: $99/year
- Google Play Developer: $25 one-time
- Screenshots design: Free (DIY) or $200-500 (hire designer)
- Total: ~$125-625 first year, then ~$100/year
