### Halal Content Guidelines

**DO:**
✅ Use Islamic greetings (As-salamu alaykum)
✅ Respectful representation of mosques, Quran
✅ Modest imagery (no inappropriate content)
✅ Ethical marketing (no deception)
✅ Culturally sensitive to all Muslims (Sunni, Shia, diverse backgrounds)
✅ Use royalty-free or halal music/sounds

**DON'T:**
❌ Music with haram lyrics
❌ Mixing genders inappropriately
❌ Disrespectful use of Quranic verses
❌ Exaggeration or false claims
❌ Engagement baiting (manipulative tactics)
❌ Culturally insensitive stereotypes

- Stay neutral on sectarian issues
- Focus on universally accepted Islamic principles
- Avoid political topics
- Don't comment on specific fatwas or scholarly disputes
