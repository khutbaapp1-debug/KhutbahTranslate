Account & Data Deletion Request

Delete your account and data

You can request deletion of your Khutbah Companion account and all associated personal data at any time.

Option 1: Email request (recommended)

  1. Send an email to Khutbah.app1@gmail.com
  2. Use subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days

Option 2: In-app deletion

  1. Open the Khutbah Companion app
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

What data is deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently delete:

  • Your account profile (name, email, profile image)
  • Authentication identifiers
  • Your saved sermons, notes, and translations
  • Khutbah implementation guidelines you created
  • Missed prayer (Qada) tracking history
  • Favorite hadiths and duas
  • App usage analytics tied to your account
  • User preferences and notification settings

What data is retained

After account deletion, we retain only:

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics (cannot be linked back to you) — retained indefinitely for service improvement
  • Records required by law (e.g., financial records related to ad revenue) — retained for up to 7 years where legally required

No personally identifiable information is retained after the deletion is complete.

Questions

For any questions about your data or this deletion process, contact us at Khutbah.app1@gmail.com.
